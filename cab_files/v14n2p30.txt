CANADIAN ARCHITECT AND BUILDER STUDENTS' COMPETITIONS.



The committee appointed, by the Ontario Association of Architects to judge the merits of the designs submitted in these competitions have reported as follows 

COMPETITION FOR VESTIBULE



'Victor" is placed first. This is a very commendable design in Italian Renaissance. The disposition of the somewhat luxurious features of its design, the plan, heights of doors, impost moldings, etc., are correctly worked out in their relation to each other. We think the author is to be commended for having taken good advantage of the fact that there was no limit of cost
