THE discovery is reported that serpentine Fire-Resisting  rock, when powdered and mixed



with good lime, forms a mortar that will successfully withstand heat to 1,100° F., and will not crack if subjected when at this heat to contact with cold water. The new material, besides being- in the highest degree fire-resisting, is also declared to be so elastic as not to crack when the building settles and permits of nails being driven into it. It is said to he one-third lighter than ordinary mortar, easy to handle, and capable of receiving decoration.
